Jacki Bolt

Aspiring Freelance Web Designer

Background

Jacki is headed toward a career change, moving from a decade of coaching high-level college field hockey toward freelance web design.

Jacki's career change is motivated by a desire to take back control of her calendar, and to be challenged in new, creative ways, while learning about and helping clients.

Goals

Jacki loves learning about her clients - their brand, their needs and their story - and then building a product that that visually represents the client well, functions in a way that improves their brand, and helps the client connect more effectively to the world around them.
